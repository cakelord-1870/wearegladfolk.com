    Our leaf-patterned cutting board features gorgeous botanical leaves in a whimsical pattern. Makes for a thoughtful gift, a stunning piece of kitchen decor, and a treasured family heirloom.

    Each piece is designed with love and made by hand in our Ohio workshop from locally sourced lumber. American-made and of the highest quality, we seal each cutting board with organic food-grade mineral oil before sending it off with care to you. Proudly display the front side; cut on the back side! Hand wash only, please.

    Available in your choice of maple, cherry, or walnut wood types and on boards sized 8" x 10", 11" x 14", and 14" x 22". Select your desired size and wood type from the drop-down menu.

    Please note that each cutting board is unique and made-to-order; wood tone and grain will vary from piece to piece.
